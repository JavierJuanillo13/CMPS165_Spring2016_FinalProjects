What The Experts Recommend

Experts recommend that you consume approximately a quarter of your daily calories for breakfast. The stereotypical American breakfast as popularized by Hollywood (shown below) is surprisingly compliant with government dietary guidelines in terms of calories, but contains excessive quantities of both cholesterol and sodium.
